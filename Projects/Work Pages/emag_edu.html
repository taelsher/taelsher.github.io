Electromagnetic Education

Electromagnetic phenomena are complex, particularly to many students who are trying to get into the field. The group actively develops methods to better allow prospective students to understand, grasp, and visualize the phenomena at hand. Activities in support of this area include development of simple and guided simulation/visualization tools and hands-on demonstrations with equipment for measuring antennas, such as VNAs and anechoic chamber systems.
